# Success Indicators (KPIs)

To measure the effectiveness and growth of Motorku, we will track the following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

## 1. Product Engagement
- **Task Completion Rate:** The percentage of users who complete the "Konfirmasi Service" checklist after receiving recommendations.
- **Session Frequency:** How often users return to the app.
- **Time-Based Estimation Acceptance:** Percentage of users who confirm the app's automated odometer estimates as accurate.

## 2. Technical Performance & Accuracy
- **Load Time (LCP):** Must be under 1.5 seconds on 3G/4G networks.
- **Recommendation Accuracy Score:** Based on the "Thumbs Up / Thumbs Down" feedback loop on service recommendations. This is a critical metric to ensure the JSON rule engine improves over time without constant expert review.

## 3. Business & Growth Metrics
- **User Retention (Day 30 & Day 90):** The core metric indicating if the app is useful enough to keep around for the next service cycle.
- **Organic Virality (K-Factor):** Number of new users acquired via in-app viral hooks (e.g., sharing to unlock PDF export).
- **Affiliate CTR (Click-Through Rate):** Once implemented, the percentage of users clicking on recommended spare parts links.
