# Epic Goals & Vision

## 1. The Ultimate Long-Term Goal (Epic Goal)
To become the **de-facto digital service manual** for every motorcycle in Indonesia. Motorku aims to replace physical service books completely, serving as the central hub where riders track maintenance, understand vehicle health, and connect with trusted service providers.

## 2. The Stretch Goal (Most Extreme)
Evolve from a standalone utility into a **seamless O2O (Online-to-Offline) maintenance ecosystem**:
- **Direct Workshop Integration:** Partner with major authorized workshops (e.g., AHASS, Yamaha Bengkel Resmi) and vetted independent garages.
- **Automated Telematics:** Integrate with modern motorcycle OBD (On-Board Diagnostics) or Bluetooth modules to automatically read odometer data and engine health, triggering predictive maintenance alerts without manual user input.

## 3. Guiding Principles for Growth
- Always prioritize the rider's understanding over technical jargon.
- Maintain a lightweight footprint (PWA-friendly, offline-capable).
- Stay strictly within the motorcycle maintenance niche (do not pivot to ride-hailing or general e-commerce).
