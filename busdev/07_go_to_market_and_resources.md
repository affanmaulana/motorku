# Go-To-Market & Resource Needs

## 1. Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy (Near-Zero Budget)
- **In-App Viral Hooks (Sharing Incentives):** Build growth directly into the product. Offer premium features (e.g., "Export Service History to PDF") for free if the user shares Motorku on WhatsApp or social media.
- **Community Engagement:** Introduce the app in local Indonesian Facebook groups and forums dedicated to specific motorcycle models (e.g., Vario 160 Riders, NMAX Indonesia).
- **Content Marketing (Social Media):** Create short-form videos (TikTok, Instagram Reels) showing the "pain" of forgetting an oil change versus the ease of checking Motorku. Focus on cost savings.
- **Low-Effort Offline Activation:** Provide free, downloadable QR code poster templates inside the app. Enthusiastic users and independent mechanics can print and stick them at their own workshops, creating decentralized, free advertising.

## 2. Resource Needs (Solo Developer / Small Team)
- **Development & Design:** 1 Solo Developer handling React + Vite. 
- **Hosting / Infrastructure:** 
  - Vercel or Netlify for free frontend hosting.
  - Edge/Serverless computing (Firebase/Supabase) to keep backend costs near zero during the cloud phase, subsidized by affiliate marketing.
- **Marketing Budget:** Bootstrap initially ($0), relying heavily on the in-app sharing incentives and community templates. 
- **Validation Loop:** Instead of hiring a full-time mechanic, rely on the in-app crowdsourced validation (thumbs up/down) to iteratively refine the service logic.
